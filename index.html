It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain that was founded in Seattle, Washington in 1971. The company is known for its premium coffee and tea beverages, as well as its pastries and other food offerings. Starbucks has over 32,000 stores in 83 countries around the world, making it one of the largest coffeehouse chains in the world.
